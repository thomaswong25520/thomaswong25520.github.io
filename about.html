I am currently a Technical Account Manager at Reputation. I have a strong foundation in cloud technologies (Python, SQL, AWS, Terraform) and a focus on building long-term, value-driven partnerships.

I work closely with product, engineering, and go-to-market teams to ensure our clients — including multi-million ARR accounts — not only adopt our solutions effectively, but also unlock their full strategic potential. My approach is collaborative: I see clients not just as users, but as partners.

I aim to understand their business goals, technical constraints, and future roadmap, so we can co-design solutions that are reliable, scalable, and aligned with long-term success. I’ve led post-launch programs, mitigated risks, and contributed to infrastructure and automation initiatives that improve both technical performance and customer experience. In parallel, I contribute to open-source projects focused on data architecture, cost efficiency, and resilient cloud infrastructure — with the goal of sharing practical tools that help teams scale more efficiently and sustainably.

Whenever possible, I try to open source the code I write on my Github profile.
